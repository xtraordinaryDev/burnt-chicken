What Makes It Different

This Is The
Real Bird

Burnt Chicken started as a pop-up with a simple mission: serve fried chicken that actually tastes like something. Cajun-spiced, crispy-battered, made to order — every single time. Now rooted on Payne Ave in Saint Paul, the mission hasn't changed.

🌶️

Cajun / Creole Roots

Every piece is seasoned deep with the spice tradition of Louisiana. Bold layers of flavor, not just heat — from the brine to the crust.

🍗

Crispy Bird, Always

Chicken thighs, hand-battered and fried to that perfect crunch. We choose thighs because they're richer, juicier, and hold up to our sauces.

🔥

Made to Order

Nothing sits under a heat lamp. When you order, we move. That's the only way to respect the bird — and the customer.
